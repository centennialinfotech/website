Remote Sales Operations Specialist

Remote Sales Operations Specialist

Join a forward-thinking company that champions excellence. This remote role is perfect for both experienced sales professionals and ambitious newcomers. Enjoy flexibility, exceptional support, and the opportunity to boost your career.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Engage with expert mentors and thrive in a supportive team environment
  • Connect with prospective clients via phone to understand their financial needs.
  • Organize and conduct virtual consultations (via Zoom or phone) for comprehensive discussions.
  • Execute persuasive presentations to close sales and offer tailored solutions.
  • Craft personalized insurance quotes tailored to individual client needs.
What We Offer:
  • A balanced 3-4 day workweek for optimal work-life harmony.
  • Free, extensive online training and ongoing support.
  • Work exclusively with warm leads, no cold calling required.
  • Prompt commission payouts with minimal waiting periods.
  • Advanced sales technology provided at no cost.
  • Continuous professional development with mentorship from industry veterans.
  • All-expenses-paid annual incentive trips to top-tier global destinations.
This is a 1099 commission-based role specializing in financial products such as IULs, annuities, and life insurance, perfect for those looking to expand their expertise and impact.

Ready to transform your sales career? Apply now and join a team dedicated to your professional growth and success .

Application Notice: U.S. Candidates Only

We are currently accepting applications only from candidates residing in the United States. Please note that any applications submitted from outside the United States will not be considered, and no further notification will be provided regarding the rejection.
